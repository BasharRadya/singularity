#### Breakdown of grading

Student grades will be weighted according to the following scheme:

| Category | Percentage |
|--|--|
| Programs | 30% |
| Exercises | 20% |
| Midpoint Presentation | 15% |
| Final Assignment + Presentation | 30% |
| Participation | 5% |
